# Car Sales Performance Dashboard (2020 - 2021)

An interactive and dynamic data analytics dashboard designed to track, visualize, and analyze car sales performance data across consecutive years (2020 and 2021). This project provides valuable business insights into sales trends, regional performance, customer preferences, and profit growth.

## 📊 Live Preview / Screenshot
![Car Sales Dashboard](Car%20Sales%20Dashboard.png)

## 💡 Key Features & Insights
* **Year-To-Date (YTD) Performance Metrics:** 
  * **Total Cars Sold:** 13,261 units (+25% YoY growth).
  * **Total Sales Revenue:** $371M (+24% YoY growth).
  * **Average Price:** $27,991 (-1% YoY).
* **Interactive Slicers:** Dynamic filtering by **Year** (2020, 2021), **Transmission** (Auto, Manual), and **Car Color** (Black, Pale White, Red).
* **Trend Analysis:** 
  * **YTD Sales By Week:** A continuous line chart showcasing the steady weekly growth of sales.
  * **Profit Breakdown Trend:** A detailed waterfall chart tracking cumulative monthly profit and comparing total annual performance between 2020 and 2021.
* **Geographical Insights:** Bar chart analyzing top-performing cities (e.g., Austin, Janesville, Scottsdale).
* **Customer Preference Segmentation:** Donut charts breaking down sales share by **Transmission Type** (52% Auto vs. 48% Manual) and **Car Color** (47% Pale White, 34% Black, 19% Red).
